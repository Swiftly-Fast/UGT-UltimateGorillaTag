Gamemode

Starting Positions: Runners start wherever they want, taggers start on top of Gazebo. When runners are ready, the referee will say "runners run" giving the runners 3 seconds to run, then the referee will say "taggers tag" releasing the taggers to go tag the runners as fast as they can.

Match Duration: The team that gets the longest run time wins the round, all matches go to a score of 5 points (unless a team needs to go to 3 points, then the opposing team will have to agree, if not it's force 5 points). Time cap is 3 minutes.
