Starting Positions

Runner team chooses where they start in forest, tagger team starts on gazebo. When the runner team is ready, the referee will say "runners run," giving the runners 3 seconds to run. After the 3 seconds, the referee will say "taggers tag," releasing the taggers. The tagger team's task is to tag the runner team as quickly as possible, while the runner team's task is to evade tagging for as long as possible.

Match Duration

The team with the longest run time wins the round after both teams have run and tagged. Matches go first to five points, or first to three points if both teams agree. Timecap for each round/match is always 3 minutes.
