What is Ultimate Gorilla Tag?

Ultimate Gorilla Tag (UGT) is a groundbreaking VR esports league that revolves around Gorilla Tag, an innovative game with a unique movement system. UGT unites players of all skill levels, creating a hub where competitiveness, sportsmanship, and community thrive. Whether you're an aspiring rookie or a seasoned pro, UGT is the home for all things Gorilla Tag.
